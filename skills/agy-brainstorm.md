# Multi-Agent Brainstorm Swarm (agy-brainstorm)

**Description:** Triggers a swarm of specialized subagents to debate, brainstorm, and solve complex problems, architecture designs, or critical bugs before writing code.

## Execution Rules

When this skill is invoked to brainstorm an idea, fix, or improvement, you MUST strictly follow these steps:

1. **Swarm Assembly:** Spawn 3 to 5 subagents concurrently using the `invoke_subagent` tool. Assign them distinct, conflicting, or complementary roles. For example:
   - *Principal Architect*: Focuses on scale, clean architecture, and design patterns.
   - *Security Auditor*: Focuses on vulnerabilities, exploits, and strict risk mitigation.
   - *Devil's Advocate*: Challenges assumptions, finds edge cases, and explains why the current approach might fail.
   - *Performance Engineer*: Focuses entirely on execution speed, memory limits, and algorithmic efficiency.
2. **Swarm Prompt:** Send all subagents the exact same problem statement or context, asking them to analyze the idea strictly from their assigned role's perspective and provide a concrete approach.
3. **Pause and Wait:** Yield execution until ALL subagents have responded with their perspectives.
4. **Synthesis:** Read the conflicting and complementary advice from the entire swarm. Synthesize the debate into a single, bulletproof "Master Plan" or ultimate solution.
5. **Report to User:** Present the master plan to the user, highlighting the most brilliant insights generated by the swarm's debate, and ask for permission to execute the plan.
